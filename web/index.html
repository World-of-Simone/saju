Your luck pillars unfold in a direction the classical system derives from your birth-year polarity together with gender — and the tradition only recognizes two. Choose the one you most identify with, or try each and compare.

Saju is an interpretive art, a way of contemplating life — not a deterministic prediction or a fixed verdict about who you are.
